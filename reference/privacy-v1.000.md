## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and deliver a more consistent experience.

Place of Interest Type Foundry uses cookies on poi.tf in accordance with applicable law.

## Types of Cookies We Use

— Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled as they enable core features such as the shopping cart and secure checkout.

— Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting anonymous usage data. We use this information to improve the site experience.

— Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices (such as language or display preferences) so you do not need to re-enter them on subsequent visits.

— Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our campaigns. These are only set if you have provided your consent.
